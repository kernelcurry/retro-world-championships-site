1st Place: GameOn Expo 2023 - Retro Championships Belt

The 2023 Retro Championships is here again! This year we're bringing you a new twist on our gauntlet format. We're going to be using each of the championship carts from 1990-1995. That's Nintendo World Championships 1990, Campus Challenge 1991, Campus Challenge 1992, Star Fox Super Weekend, Powerfest 94' and Donkey Kong Country Competition. Dorion and Mike have been working on this idea for a while and are really excited to bring this to you. This year marks the 30th anniversary of Stafox Super Weekend so that will be our qualifier. The other 5 carts will be part of our finals gauntlet to determine who becomes the 2023 Game On Expo Retro Champion and takes home the title belt.
