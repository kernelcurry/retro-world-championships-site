The year at Game On Expo: Retro Championships 2018 we have another exciting lineup of tournament games for you, and an extra special guest!

Back in 1994, Sega has their World Championships events, the Sega Rock the Rock World Championships! This was a very exciting time for Sega, in the height of their popularity. Sega offered a massive $25,000 grab prize, and the tournament was shown on MTV, in the time when Video Games were still trying to find their place in pop culture!

Our featured guest will be none other than Chris Tang, the Sega World Champion himself! Chris was also a finalist in the 1990 Nintendo World Championships, and continues to stay active in the gaming community commentating on events like The Classic Tetris World Championships!

In honor of the Sega World Championships, we will be doing a Sega Genesis inspired tournament this year, once again encompassing come of the most popular games across multiple genres.
